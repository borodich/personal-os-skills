# Outreach Templates

Personalized templates for reaching out to founders. Always customize based on their specific project.

## Template 1: GitHub Discovery

**Subject:** Saw {repo_name} trending — quick question

```
Hey {founder_name},

Noticed {repo_name} on GitHub trending — {specific_detail_from_code_or_readme}.

I'm with AINative Capital, we focus on AI infrastructure and developer tools at pre-seed/seed. 

{why_interesting} makes this particularly interesting to us.

Would you be open to a quick 15-min call this week? No pitch needed — just curious about your plans.

Best,
{sender}
```

## Template 2: HackerNews Show HN

**Subject:** Re: Show HN: {project_name}

```
Hi {founder_name},

Great Show HN post — {specific_comment_or_feature_they_mentioned}.

We're AINative Capital, AI-first VC. Your approach to {specific_problem} caught our attention because {reasoning}.

Any chance you're exploring funding? Happy to share thoughts even if not.

{sender}
```

## Template 3: Building in Public

**Subject:** Following your progress on {project}

```
{founder_name},

Been following your {twitter/blog} posts about building {project}. The update about {specific_recent_milestone} was impressive.

We invest in AI-native companies at early stage. Your trajectory matches what we look for.

Open to connecting? No pressure on timing — happy to be a resource whenever helpful.

{sender}
```

## Template 4: Warm via Code

**Subject:** Impressed by your {language} work

```
Hey {founder_name},

Was looking through {repo_name}'s codebase — really clean implementation of {specific_technical_detail}. 

I lead investments at AINative Capital. We're thesis-driven around AI infrastructure, and your approach to {problem_space} is exactly the kind of technical depth we back.

Would love to learn more about where you're taking this. 15 minutes?

{sender}
```

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to do)

❌ "We're a VC looking to invest in exciting startups..."
❌ Generic praise without specifics
❌ Long emails with fund thesis
❌ Asking for pitch deck immediately
❌ CC'ing multiple people

## Aura VC (Semantic VC)
  > Hi Alex — [YOUR NAME] here. Semantic version control for AI coding agents... that's what the stack needs to scale reliably. I have a background in [YOUR BACKGROUND], now invest in early-stage AI infrastructure. Are you raising a follow-on to the a16z round? I'd love to chat about what you’re seeing on the tooling adoption curve and whether we can be helpful as board members or tech partners. Congrats again on shipping a critical piece of infrastructure! [LinkedIn profile URL]


Before sending, verify:
- [ ] Used their actual name (check spelling)
- [ ] Referenced specific detail from their project
- [ ] Explained why THIS project (not generic)
- [ ] Kept under 100 words
- [ ] Clear single ask (call, not "let's chat sometime")
- [ ] No typos in their project name
